Canon EOS 3000D DSLR Camera

The Canon EOS 3000D is an entry-level DSLR camera perfect for beginners looking to step into the world of photography. It features an 18.0 megapixel APS-C CMOS sensor and Canon's DIGIC 4+ image processor, delivering stunning image quality and performance.

Specifications

Feature Details
Image Sensor 18.0 Megapixel APS-C CMOS
Processor DIGIC 4+
ISO Range 100-6400 (expandable to 12800)
Video Recording Full HD 1080p @ 30fps
